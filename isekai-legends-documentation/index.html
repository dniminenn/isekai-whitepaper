Introduction

Welcome to the Isekai Legends documentation! This guide will provide you with an overview of the project, including its tokenomics, waifus, adventurers’ guilds, reward distribution mechanisms, and referral system.

Isekai Legends is a decentralized platform that offers an engaging and rewarding experience for users who love anime and NFTs. In Isekai Legends, players can visit the Sanctum of Legends to mint powerful and rare unique waifus or explore the Garden of Seasons to discover enchanting seasonal waifus through a gacha-like experience. These waifus can be combined into formidable parties at the Arcane Party Assembler, which creates uniform farmable tokens based on their rarity and power.

Armed with their newly formed parties, players can join the Order of Legendaries and Seasonal Enclave guilds to participate in various farming activities and earn rewards in the form of the ERC20 token $ISEKAI. The Order of Legendaries caters to unique waifu parties, granting perpetual rewards, while the Seasonal Enclave offers seasonal rewards for seasonal waifu parties.

Every season ushers in a new era of farming rewards with an assortment of farmable Seasonal Waifus. Each season offers fresh and captivating art, engaging lore, and powerful abilities for the Waifus, providing players with an evolving and immersive experience. The seasonal nature of the protocol keeps it dynamic and exciting, as players eagerly anticipate the unique opportunities and challenges that come with each new season.

Get ready to embark on a thrilling journey into the world of Isekai Legends!

Tokenomics

The Isekai token ($ISEKAI) serves as the core currency in the Isekai Legends ecosystem.

  • 100% (69T) allocated for liquidity
  • 10% tax with exclusion for selling and creating LP
    • Used to fund rewards seasons for Adventurers Guilds
    • 1% for protocol development
  • 0% dev allocation
    • All tokens were minted to initial liquidity pool of $1000USD

Funding Rewards

The tax cannot be raised by the team, the contract ownership has been renounced.

The tax can only be decremented until it reaches ZERO, effectively burning the tax.

A adjustable tax mechanism is in place for $ISEKAI token transactions, with the exception of sells to our Sushiswap LP. This tax is utilized to fund the farming rewards for each season, ensuring a continuous flow of rewards. The $ISEKAI tax is 10% at protocol launch. This tax is combined with the mint proceeds from the previous season to fund the next season of rewards.

The following transactions are free:

  • Selling on Sushiswap
  • Adding liquidity on Sushiswap
  • Transfering funds from wallet to wallet (excluding contract wallets)

The following transactions are taxed:

  • Buying $ISEKAI anywhere, including Sushiswap
  • Claiming $ISEKAI rewards
  • Breaking LP anywhere, including Sushiswap

Since $ISEKAI is a fee on transfer token, slippage on your Decentralized Exchange (DEX) must be set to TAX + SLIPPAGE TOLERANCE in the DEX UI when buying $ISEKAI on the market.

For example: if your tolerance is 0.5%, then raise slippage to 10.5% to allow the transaction to execute while preventing frontrunning.

Funding Protocol Development

A small percentage (1%) of the tax collected from $ISEKAI token transactions is allocated to fund the ongoing development of the Isekai Legends protocol. This ensures that the dApp continues to evolve.

Autobuy Isekai Token with Mint Proceeds

Along with $ISEKAI tax, mint proceeds are also used to fund farming rewards.

To maintain a sustainable reward system, the mint proceeds from the Isekai ecosystem are programmatically set to autobuy $ISEKAI tokens. These tokens, combined with the proceeds from the Garden of Seasons, are then used to fund the next round of rewards for the Order of Legendaries and Seasonal Enclave guilds, creating a robust and self-sustaining economy within the Isekai Legends ecosystem.

By integrating the Isekai token into various aspects of the dApp, Isekai Legends ensures that its currency plays a pivotal role in facilitating player engagement and supporting the overall growth of the platform.

Waifus

In Isekai Legends, Waifus are the central characters that players collect, treasure, and utilize for various in-game activities. Waifus are divided into two main categories: Unique Waifus and Seasonal Waifus. Each category offers distinct features and rarities that cater to different preferences and play styles. Before staking, Waifus must be wrapped into a party, forming their combined “Party Power Levels.”

Sanctum of Legends

Unique Waifus are rare and highly sought-after characters with a limited supply. They boast a strong collectible value and are prized possessions among players. Unique Waifus are available in two rarities, each with their own farming power multiplier:

  • Epic: Exceptionally powerful and rare characters with a 1500x farming power multiplier.
  • Legendary: The pinnacle of rarity and power in the Isekai Legends universe with a 3000x farming power multiplier.

As the Isekai Legends universe expands, new Unique Waifus will be periodically introduced to the Sanctum of Legends. These releases offer players the chance to discover and collect new, exclusive waifus with unique abilities and designs, adding to the excitement and sense of discovery within.

Garden of Seasons

The Garden of Seasons is a special area within the Isekai Legends universe where players can mint Seasonal Waifus. These waifus are a rotating collection of captivating characters with unique abilities, artwork, and stories. Each season brings a fresh array of waifus, offering players the excitement of discovering and collecting new and exclusive characters. Limited-Time Availability Seasonal Waifus are only available for a predetermined period, which corresponds to the duration of each season. Once a season ends, the associated waifus become unattainable, making them highly collectible and valuable to players who managed to obtain them during the season.

The price for minting Seasonal Waifus is 10 $MATIC, which may change in subsequent seasons. Seasonal Waifus may also be minted for 1 $CRYSTAL.

Seasonal Waifus offer a variety of rarities, allowing players to collect and treasure a diverse range of characters. Each rarity has its own farming power multiplier:

  • Common: 1x farming power multiplier.
  • Uncommon: 5x farming power multiplier.
  • Rare: 20x farming power multiplier.
  • Epic: 100x farming power multiplier.
  • Legendary: 1500x farming power multiplier.

The higher the rarity of a Seasonal Waifu, the greater the farming power multiplier, allowing players to earn more rewards when staking them in the Seasonal Enclave Adventurers’ Guild.

Forming a Party - Arcane Party Assembler

In the world of Isekai Legends, the Arcane Party Assembler plays a crucial role in uniting adventurers and their waifus for exciting quests and thrilling battles. This mystical process allows players to transform their Unique and Seasonal Waifus into wrapped waifus, called Power Level facilitating a more streamlined and efficient distribution of rewards across different rarities and multipliers.

Harnessing the Power of Arcane Magic

Through the use of ancient arcane magic, the Arcane Party Assembler converts Unique and Seasonal Waifus separately into wrapped waifus, imbuing them with a unified energy form. This mystical transformation allows for a more balanced distribution of rewards, taking into account the various rarities and multipliers of the waifus.

Party Power Level

The number of wrapped waifus obtained from the Arcane Party Assembler reflects their rarity multiplier and establishes the power level of each party. For instance, a party with 3000 wrapped waifu tokens possesses a power level of 3000. This power level acts as a gauge of a party's prowess and potential within the Isekai Legends universe.

Embarking on Exciting Adventures

By utilizing the Arcane Party Assembler, players can prepare their waifu collection for the thrilling quests and challenges that lie ahead. As they explore the enchanting world of Isekai Legends, their parties of wrapped waifus stand ready to face epic battles, overcome obstacles, and earn valuable rewards.

Future Potential for Expired Parties

The Arcane Party Assembler also holds the promise of future functionality for parties that have completed their seasonal adventures. These expired parties may unlock special benefits, such as:

  • Exclusive NFT collectibles representing their past achievements and adventures.
  • Eligibility for bounties, granting players access to unique in-game content or additional rewards.
  • Entry into a lottery, providing a chance to win rare waifus or other valuable prizes.

While the specifics of these features are still in development, they aim to enhance the Isekai Legends experience and reward players for their continued dedication and engagement.

The Arcane Party Assembler provides an essential tool for players as they navigate the vast world of Isekai Legends, ensuring a smooth and engaging experience for every adventurer. The arcane magic that powers the Assembler not only streamlines reward distribution but also adds a touch of mystical allure to the overall journey.

Adventurers’ Guilds

Isekai Legends features two distinguished Adventurers’ Guilds that players can join to stake their wrapped Waifu parties’ Party Power Levels and earn $ISEKAI tokens as rewards. Staking your Party Power Levels into the guilds is akin to “sending your Waifus on an adventure.” The guilds cater to the different Waifu categories, providing a tailored experience for players based on their collections.

The Protocol Income from the $ISEKAI tax and seasonal mints is divided among these guilds.

Order of Legendaries

The Order of Legendaries is an esteemed society that caters exclusively to players who hold Unique Waifus. By staking their Party Power Levels comprised of Unique Waifus, players gain access to a prestigious and rewarding experience. The Order of Legendaries receives a portion of the protocol income, which is allocated to reward its members.

Seasonal Enclave

The Seasonal Enclave is a vibrant and ever-changing community tailored for players with Seasonal Waifus. As players stake their Party Power Levels comprised of Seasonal Waifus, they can earn a share of the protocol income allocated for the Seasonal Enclave.

Adventure Rewards

The rewards are calculated based on the staked Party Power Levels. The contract maintains a fixed amount of total rewards distributed per block, and keeps track of the total staked Party Power Levels in the guild. When a user stakes Party Power Levels, the contract updates the reward accumulation rate based on the elapsed time and the total staked Party Power Levels. The reward accumulation rate is increased proportionally to the new rewards, which are calculated as the product of the number of blocks since the last update and the reward per block, divided by the total staked Party Power Levels.

The contract calculates the pending rewards for a user by first determining the number of blocks since their last action. It then computes the new rewards and the new accumulation rate based on the elapsed blocks. The pending rewards for the user are the difference between the accumulated rewards (the product of the user's staked Party Power Levels and the new accumulation rate) and the user's reward debt. The reward debt concept is crucial in ensuring fair reward distribution among users. It keeps track of the rewards that a user has already claimed or is entitled to up until their last interaction with the contract. The reward debt serves as a reference point to calculate the user's new pending rewards accurately. When a user stakes or withdraws Party Power Levels, or claims rewards, the contract calculates the user's pending rewards based on the difference between their accumulated rewards and their reward debt. The accumulated rewards are calculated using the user's staked Party Power Levels and the updated reward accumulation rate. By considering the reward debt, the contract ensures that users receive rewards only for the time period after their last interaction with the contract. This prevents users from double-dipping or receiving rewards they have already claimed. Additionally, the reward debt helps maintain fairness in the reward distribution, as users who stake more Party Power Levels or interact with the contract more frequently will have their rewards calculated accurately based on their contribution to the total staked Party Power Levels. In summary, the reward debt is a critical component in the reward calculation mechanism, which allows for a fair distribution of rewards among users based on their staked Party Power Levels and interactions with the contract.

This mechanism ensures that rewards are distributed fairly among the users based on their staked Party Power Levels, and that the reward accumulation rate is adjusted every time someone interacts with the contract, accurately accounting for the rewards per time period. Furthermore, this method guarantees that all the rewards are distributed at the end of the season.

The Crystals Token

The Crystals token is a special utility token exclusive to the Isekai Legends ecosystem, designed specifically for rewarding Liquidity Providers (LPs). Crystals are untradable and untransferable, ensuring that their value is tied directly to the Isekai Legends platform.

$CRYSTALS can be used to mint Seasonal Waifus at a redemption ratio of 1:1 at protocol launch.

Rewarding LP Farmers

The Crystal Treasure Finder is a unique mechanism that allows players to stake their LP tokens and earn rewards in the form of Crystals. These rewards are minted by the LP rewarder, creating an incentive for players to contribute liquidity to the platform.

Spending Crystals on Seasonal Waifus

Crystals can only be spent on minting seasonal waifus at the Garden of Seasons. Players who use Crystals to mint seasonal waifus will enjoy better rarity drop rates compared to those who mint using MATIC. This advantage encourages participation in the LP farming system and creates a more vibrant ecosystem within Isekai Legends.

$CRYSTALS do not drop COMMON waifus, only UNCOMMON and above!

However,

$CRYSTALS cannot be exchanged, transfered, sold, nor bought. They are minted by the LP farm and burned when minting Isekai Legends NFTs.

Crystals Decay Mechanism

In the mystical world of Isekai Legends, Crystals possess a unique property that causes them to decay over time. At the end of each season, the Crystals' potency diminishes by a factor of 1/2 (one half), representing their ephemeral nature.

This decay mechanism encourages players to spend their Crystals on seasonal waifus during the current season rather than hoarding them for future seasons. Although players are free to accumulate Crystals, they will find it more advantageous to utilize them within the same season they are earned.
